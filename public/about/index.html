About

Sohail Jannessari

I am currently an adjunct professor at Universitat Pompeu Fabra. Since 2017 I’ve been teaching quantitative methods courses to the undergraduate students of political science. You can find the syllabi here.

Over the last ten years I have, in different capacities, collaborated with all sorts of media outlets around the world, from proofreading for Persian media to being interviewed about covid situation by CBC, to writing op-eds for Catarsi magazin here in Catalonia. You can find some of my journalistic work here.

Most of my work is focused on quantitative studies of elections and parties, but I also dabble in international relations from time to time.
